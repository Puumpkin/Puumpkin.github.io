Problem: You enter a room and are met with a spring block system that has a block that's gripped by a claw attached to a spring which is at equilibrium. The goal of the block is to be pushed with enough force to stop on a certain point. Your goal is to find the displacement the spring needs to be pulled back from equilibrium to reach a certain distance. You are given the mass of the block in kilograms, the coefficient of friction, the spring constant, the gravitational constant, and the distance that needs to be traveled in meters.
